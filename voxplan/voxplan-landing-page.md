## The Problem with Traditional Task Apps

You've tried them all. The to-do lists that grow endlessly. The project managers that require a PhD to operate. The habit trackers that guilt you into submission.

**They all miss the point.**

Goals aren't flat lists. They're hierarchies. "Learn guitar" breaks down into "master chords," which breaks down into "practice F chord." Real achievement requires this natural decomposition ‚Äî but without overwhelming complexity.

**Time is your only real constraint.** You can have unlimited goals, but you only have 24 hours. Most apps ignore this fundamental truth. VoxPlan doesn't.

## FAQ

### Is VoxPlan really free?
Yes. The core app is free and always will be. Future cloud sync features may have a Pro tier.

### Why isn't it on Google Play yet?
Direct APK distribution is faster and gives you the app immediately. Play Store is coming soon.

### Does it sync with other apps?
Not yet. VoxPlan is designed to be your single source of truth for goals. Export features are planned.

### Can I contribute?
Absolutely! The code is on GitHub. We welcome bug reports, feature ideas, and pull requests.

### What about iOS?
Android first, but iOS is on the roadmap for late 2026.

### How is this different from Forest/Pomodoro apps?
Those apps focus on single sessions. VoxPlan connects those sessions to your actual goals and tracks long-term progress.
